From Paper With Tasks

Motivation: As I was going through the paperswithcode.com (where I colldected the data from) and reading the different abstracts of diiferent papers and eyeing the task section of that paper, I was struck with an idea that, what if I buil a classification model, where I will put the abstract of a paper and it will output the task that can be done using the idea of that paper, or sort of a recommendation system where I will be able to know what can be done using the idea of the paper. Going with this idea, I started to collect data from the paperswithcode.com through scraping and built my own From Paper With Tasks. Inspired by the movie named From Paris With Love.
